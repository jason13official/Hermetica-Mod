# Hermetica

> The study of magic, the natural world, and manipulating them (with consequences).

## How did we (the player) get here?

We're not sure... this world seems completely foreign to the one we came from, but there's still a tingle of the same
power we were able to control before. We'll need to investigate the magic of this land, and see what we can remember
from our studies to find a way back home.

## How do we discover magic?

Far on the horizon we can see a familiar glow, faint dots dispersed among the grass of the great plains biome we woke
up in. Our teachers called these Aura Nodes, and they can give us a sort of link to the ethereal plane where magic
resides.

## How do we learn to exploit magic?

Magic in this realm is severely weakened compared to where we came from. Our typical "Sparks" spell which
was previously capable of incinerating zombies now only deals a mild amount of damage from a form of heat wave.
It's a bit worrisome, and I'm scared when I get back home my magic will still be weaker. I'll need to advance my
knowledge and even somehow augment my spell or casting tool to get back to prime.
